WHY NOW
The PRD-153 → 154 → 155 closeout arc concluded that the Moomoo
Statement Consumer produces no joinable signal. `docs/audit_doctrine.md`
(PRD-155, 2026-05-23) codified that `logs/audit.jsonl` is structurally
a ~1-record-per-pipeline-invocation stream — the consumer was built
assuming intraday audit-write coverage that does not exist and, per
Rule 1 of the new doctrine, will not exist absent a named consumer.
The subsystem is dead code; pull it before its presence misleads.

MAX EXPECTED DELTA
Pure deletion. ~720 LOC removed from cuttingboard/ + tests/ (3
production modules + 3 test modules + 1 fixture generator + 1 PDF +
1 logs file + 1 reports/ directory). ≤10 LOC modified in pyproject.toml.
No new production LOC.

GOAL
Remove the entire PRD-153 Moomoo subsystem (production modules, tests,
fixtures, generated artifacts, dependencies) without touching the
independently-valuable artifacts of PRD-154 (`logs/audit.jsonl` scrub)
or PRD-155 (`docs/audit_doctrine.md` + consumer annotations).

SCOPE
- Delete the three `cuttingboard/moomoo_*.py` production modules.
- Delete the three `tests/test_moomoo_*.py` test modules.
- Delete `tests/fixtures/moomoo/` in full (synthetic PDF + generator).
- Delete `logs/moomoo_review.jsonl` and `reports/moomoo/`.
- Remove `pdfplumber` and `reportlab` from `pyproject.toml`.
- Flip PRD-153 status to DEPRECATED with a pointer to PRD-156.
- Append a lesson entry to `docs/DECISIONS.md`.

OUT OF SCOPE
- Any refactoring of remaining `cuttingboard/` code.
- Any change to `docs/audit_doctrine.md`, `cuttingboard/audit.py`, or
  `logs/audit.jsonl` (PRD-154/155 outputs).

  `real-data-validation` skill and `scripts/validate_consumer_prd.py`
  reference `private/<source>/` as a generic convention. The line's

- `scripts/validate_consumer_prd.py` — generic CONSUMER-class harness;
  the moomoo references in its docstring are illustrative, not coupling.
