# PRD-154 — Scrub historical pytest contamination from logs/audit.jsonl

STATUS
IN PROGRESS

LANE
MICRO

GOAL
`logs/audit.jsonl` carries 72 pipeline-shaped records whose `report_path` points to pytest tmpdirs. They are historical residue from runs predating the two test-isolation guards now in place at HEAD: per-test monkey-patch at `tests/test_runtime_decision.py:115` (commit `a46a792`, 2026-05-09) and suite-wide autouse fixture `_isolate_real_log_paths` at `tests/conftest.py:22` (commit `8b4e654`, 2026-05-10). New contamination cannot land; the residue itself contaminates every downstream consumer of the log — surfaced when PRD-153's `moomoo_review` real-data validation on 2026-05-22 found 72 of 77 pipeline records were pytest fixtures. Scrub the 72 polluting records; retain the 5 genuine production pipeline records and all 609 notification-event records.

SCOPE
- Remove every line from `logs/audit.jsonl` whose parsed record has `report_path` containing `/pytest-` (matches both `pytest-of-runner` and `pytest-of-dustin` paths).
- Retain every notification-event record (`event == "notification"`) — these are never produced by tests under the autouse fixture and are independent of the contamination.
- Retain the 5 genuine pipeline records (timestamps `2026-05-07T05:22Z`, `2026-05-12T04:13Z`, `2026-05-12T11:32Z`, `2026-05-12T20:55Z`, `2026-05-13T11:25Z`; all with `report_path` of the form `reports/YYYY-MM-DD.md`).
- Commit the scrubbed file as a one-shot hygiene fix.
- Track this PRD in registry/state per existing convention.
