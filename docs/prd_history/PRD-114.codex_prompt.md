### Mandatory challenges

Address each of the following twelve challenges. For each, state
**ACCEPT / REJECT / MODIFY** with one paragraph of reasoning and one
taxonomy label.

1. **CLASS challenge.** Should this be SIDECAR or CONSUMER? PRD-114
   produces an artifact and has no consumer in v1. Defend or
   reclassify.
2. **`watch_reason` and `event_risk` semantics.** Claude's review
   drops `event_risk` and freezes `watch_reason` to static text. Is
   this sufficient to prevent `threshold→label synthesis`? Or should
   `watch_reason` be removed entirely from v1 to eliminate any
   ambiguity?
3. **Duplication with `market_map`.** Does PRD-114 duplicate
   `market_map` grading or `trend_structure_snapshot` semantics in
   any way the Claude review missed?
4. **Manual-state persistence.** Should the curated watchlist live
   in repo (as PRD-114 specifies) or as a generated/external
   artifact? Defend or reject the in-repo decision.
5. **Minimum viable schema.** Is the four-field record
   (`symbol`, `sector_theme`, `watch_reason`, `current_price`) the
   true minimum, or can it be reduced further without breaking the
   sidecar's stated purpose?
6. **Ranking-engine creep.** Does any field, ordering rule, or
   future-extension hook in PRD-114 implicitly create a ranking
   surface? Identify any latent ordering semantics.
7. **Hidden coupling.** Identify any cross-module data flow that is
   not declared via imports or `docs/artifact_flow_map.md`.
   Specifically check: does the runtime ordering imply read coupling
   on `trend_structure_snapshot`?
8. **Determinism / staleness.** The builder accepts `generated_at`
   from runtime. Confirm this matches the trend_structure pattern.
   Identify any path where output bytes could differ on identical
   inputs.
9. **FILES recommendation.** Confirm or amend the four-file FILES
   list. Specifically: should `docs/PROJECT_STATE.md` or
   `docs/PRD_REGISTRY.md` be in FILES, or are they correctly excluded
   per PRD-113 (registry/state on close excepted)?
10. **FAIL condition recommendation.** Identify any missing FAIL
    line. In particular, address Claude's F9 (HALT short-circuit
    ordering): inspect `runtime.py` and confirm whether
    `_write_watchlist_snapshot` would be reached during a HALT path

    write to be skipped on HALT.
11. **Deferred fields.** Confirm the OUT OF SCOPE list correctly
    defers vwap relation, sma relations, relative_volume,
    trend_alignment, entry_context, and event_risk to PRD-115+.
    Identify any field that should NOT be deferred (i.e. belongs in
    v1) or any deferred field that should be permanently rejected
    rather than deferred.
12. **PRD-113 failure taxonomy application.** Independently tag
    PRD-114 against the nine forbidden patterns. Identify any
