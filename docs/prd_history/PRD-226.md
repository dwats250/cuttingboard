CLASS
CONSUMER

WHY NOW
The chatgpt-codex-connector review of PR #86 (PRD-222) flagged that the level
diagram's "NOW" anchor can carry a price that is NOT the current price. The
10-PR codex-connector audit (2026-07-03) confirmed it reachable: `contract_entry`
is the contract's planned entry (`trade_candidates[].entry`) — under
`ENTRY_MODE_PULLBACK_IMBALANCE` a computed FVG-midpoint level, and in every mode
sourced from a different artifact than the market_map current price, so it
diverges on staleness. The diagram labels that entry "NOW" and measures every
level's %-distance from it. On a HIGH-RISK consumer surface this is a mislabelled
price, not a cosmetic nit.

MAX EXPECTED DELTA
45 production LOC, all in cuttingboard/delivery/dashboard_renderer.py.

GOAL
The level diagram's NOW anchor and 0% reference are the live current price; the
contract's planned entry is shown as a distinct ENTRY level (and the risk-band
top edge), never relabelled NOW.

SCOPE
- R1: `_render_level_diagram` takes the current price (`now_price`) as the NOW
  anchor — the yellow focal line — and the 0% base for `_pct`. The contract
  entry is passed separately; it is NEVER the NOW label.
- R2: when a valid contract entry differs from NOW beyond display resolution
  (|entry − now| ≥ 0.005), it renders its own amber ENTRY line labelled
  `ENTRY <price> <±%>`. When it equals NOW (proven equal) the two coincide and
  only NOW is drawn (the current output for the equal case is unchanged).
- R3: the PRD-223 risk band stays the contract's entry→stop span (its edge is
  the contract entry, not NOW); STOP and every fib/zone/VWAP %-distance is
  measured from NOW (current price).
- R4: current-price-absent fallback — when `now_price` is unavailable but a
  valid contract entry exists, the entry is the sole anchor and is labelled
  ENTRY (never NOW); the diagram still renders on that honest anchor.

OUT OF SCOPE
- Any change to how `contract_entry_map` / `contract_stop_map` are loaded or to
  the contract artifact (`_load_contract_entry_context` is untouched).
- The contract-staleness nulling of the entry map (PRD-223) — retained as-is; a
  stale contract still drops to a current-price NOW anchor with no band.
- Redesign of the risk-band shading, STOP edge, fib/zone/VWAP styling, or the
  label-declutter pass.
- The candidate card's textual ENTRY field (`trade_framing.entry`) — unrelated
  to the SVG ENTRY marker.

REQUIREMENTS

R1 — NOW anchor and 0% reference are the current price
`_render_level_diagram` draws the yellow NOW line at `now_price` and computes
every `_pct` distance from `now_price` (the `anchor_base` when now is valid).
FAIL: for a card with `current_price=120` and `contract_entry=110`, the rendered
diagram lacks `>NOW 120.00` or a level's % is computed from 110 rather than 120.

R2 — Contract entry renders as a distinct ENTRY level when it differs from NOW
A valid contract entry differing from NOW by ≥ 0.005 renders `ENTRY <price> <±%>`
on its own amber line; an entry equal to NOW draws no separate ENTRY marker.
FAIL: `current_price=120, contract_entry=110` produces no `>ENTRY 110.00` label,
OR `current_price=100, contract_entry=100` produces an `>ENTRY ` label.

R3 — Risk band stays entry→stop; distances measured from NOW
The PRD-223 band spans the contract entry→stop y-range; STOP's %-distance is
measured from NOW.
FAIL: with `now=508, entry=510, stop=505`, the band rect does not span the
entry(510)→stop(505) y-range, or `>STOP 505.00` carries a % not equal to
`(505−508)/508` (−0.6%).

R4 — Entry is the sole, honestly-labelled anchor when current price is absent
When `now_price` is invalid but the contract entry is valid, the diagram renders
with the entry as anchor, labelled ENTRY, and no NOW line.
FAIL: with `current_price=None` and `contract_entry=110` (+ level context), the
diagram is suppressed, OR it emits a `>NOW ` label.

DATA FLOW
market_map symbol current_price → now_price → NOW line + `_pct` 0% base
contract trade_candidates[].entry → contract_entry → ENTRY line (when ≠ NOW) +
risk-band top edge → entry→stop band

FAIL CONDITIONS
- NOW label or 0% reference resolves to the contract entry when it differs from
  current price (R1).
- A differing contract entry renders no ENTRY marker, or an equal one renders a
  spurious ENTRY marker (R2).
- The risk band is measured from NOW instead of the contract entry, or STOP/level
  %-distances are measured from the entry instead of NOW (R3).
- The diagram is suppressed, or falsely labels NOW, when only the contract entry
  is available (R4).
- Any pre-existing level-diagram test fails beyond the three enumerated NOW-anchor
  updates.

VALIDATION

- Render a card where current_price ≠ contract_entry: the yellow NOW line sits at
  current price (0%), a separate amber ENTRY line sits at the contract entry with
  its signed %, and the red risk band spans entry→stop. Binary: `>NOW <cur>` and
  `>ENTRY <entry> ` both present; `>NOW <entry>` absent.
