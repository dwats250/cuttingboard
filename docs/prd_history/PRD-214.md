WHY NOW
The macro tape rendered each driver twice — once in the driver grid, then again
as a per-driver "risk-ON/OFF vote (…)" line with an italic gloss. That is ~10
restated lines that dominate the panel while adding no information the grid and
MACRO BIAS headline don't already carry.

MAX EXPECTED DELTA
40 production LOC (dashboard_renderer.py macro-tape section + _CSS + imports).

GOAL
Replace the always-expanded per-driver macro-evidence block with a single
always-visible risk-vote tally under the MACRO BIAS headline, deleting the
redundant surface outright.

SCOPE
- Add a one-line tally under the MACRO BIAS headline: risk-off count / risk-on
  count and the derived bias (e.g. "Risk votes: 3 off / 1 on → SHORT"), computed
  from the existing long_votes/short_votes counts.
- Remove the `.macro-evidence` render block and its `.macro-evidence-*` CSS.
- Drop the now-unused MACRO_BIAS_INTERPRETATION / _MACRO_BIAS_NEUTRAL_INTERP
  imports from the renderer.

OUT OF SCOPE
- Changing vote-tallying logic or the pro-/contra-cyclical classification.
- Editing macro_tape_layout.py — its interpretation constants remain as data
  (now unconsumed by the renderer; retained for potential future consumers and
  still covered by test_macro_tape_layout.py).
