three presentation nits: the distilled system-state said "no qualified

contradiction); the folded macro-pressure phrases wanted one-per-line bullets;

"no qualified candidates" for any NO_TRADE — even when `market_map` held
high-grade setups. It never consulted the market map it sits above.
