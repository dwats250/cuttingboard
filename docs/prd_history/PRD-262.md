PRD-262 — Missing/NaN pct_change fails loud (F-02): no fabricated 0.0 in ingestion, normalization, or the breadth denominator

Status: IN PROGRESS

LANE

WHY NOW
A missing/NaN previous_close or pct_change becomes a fabricated 0.0
(valid-looking calm) at two layers, silently disarming the kill-switch pct
legs, CHAOTIC override, and regime vote coloring. Characterization (below)
showed the naive fix creates a third fabrication one layer up: a shrinking
breadth denominator fabricates a ratio from absent data, loosening a live
gate. All three land together.

MAX EXPECTED DELTA
~20 production LOC across 3 files.

GOAL
Make a missing/NaN previous_close or pct_change fail loud (fetch failure /
normalization dropout) instead of fabricating a calm-looking 0.0, and make
the EXPANSION breadth denominator immune to dropout, so no stress guard or
regime gate can be silently disarmed or loosened by absent data.

SCOPE — three twins, one defect ("don't fabricate from missing input")
1. cuttingboard/ingestion.py _yfinance_quote_raw (:288): missing,
   non-finite, or <=0 previous_close raises (same pattern as the
   existing last_price guards);
   the retry wrapper _try_yfinance_quote (:235) then returns
   RawQuote(fetch_succeeded=False, failure_reason=...).
2. cuttingboard/normalization.py: NaN pct_change_raw becomes a
   normalization failure (quote dropped, ERROR log) instead of _to_decimal
   (:96) returning 0.0.
3. cuttingboard/regime.py detect_expansion_regime (:84): the breadth leg
   divides by the CONFIGURED tradable-universe size (ALL_SYMBOLS minus
   NON_TRADABLE_SYMBOLS = 16), not by the count of symbols present. A
   symbol that failed to report counts as non-advancing.

   Grounds (operator ruling (a), 2026-07-18, verbatim intent): a ratio
   gate with a shrinking denominator converts "vote against" into
   "abstain," and abstention is directional — it always helps whoever is
   winning among survivors. A symbol that failed to report did not
   advance; dropping it from the denominator asserts something the data
   does not support. F-02: don't fabricate a number when the input is
   missing. This: don't fabricate a ratio when the input is missing. The
   indivisibility argument (landing one twin leaves the fabrication alive
   at the untouched layer) applies to it exactly.

CHARACTERIZATION (Gate A addition, run 2026-07-18, pre-implementation)

Question: post-F-02, a non-halt tradable with missing data leaves
valid_quotes instead of counting as a fabricated non-advancing 0.0 vote.
Breadth (regime.py detect_expansion_regime) divides advancing/total over
the symbols PRESENT, so a dropout shrinks the denominator and can RAISE
the ratio. EXPANSION now gates a live candidate-promotion path
(PRD-259/260). Quantify the loosening on real data before writing the fix.

Method: replicated detect_expansion_regime's four legs exactly (index
alignment, VIX <= -1%, breadth >= 0.70, leadership >= 2 of the 4
in-universe leaders at >= +1.5%) over the real OHLCV cache (data/cache,
23 symbols), 247 trading days with complete data, 2025-07-11 ..
2026-07-06. Tradable universe = 16 symbols; 14 are non-halt (all but
SPY/QQQ) and can drop out silently. Adversarial dropout = removing only
genuinely non-advancing non-halt symbols (the worst case; also the case
where true full-data breadth says no).

Results:
- EXPANSION fires today on 30/247 days. The other three legs pass with
  breadth the SOLE blocker on 19 further days.
- Adversarial dropouts needed to flip those 19 days:
  N=1 flips 6 days; N<=2 flips 10; N<=4 flips 16; N<=6 flips 19 (all).
- ONE dark symbol is enough to inflate EXPANSION frequency by +6 days/yr
  (+20% over today's 30), on days where TRUE full-data breadth fails.
- Realistic single-symbol outages flip real days each: USO 5, META 4,
  AMZN 4, PAAS 3, AAPL 3, GLD/SLV/GDX/XLE 2 each, IWM/NVDA/COIN 1 each.
- Unbounded in the limit: with maximal dropout (survivors = SPY, QQQ +
  2 advancing leaders) breadth is 4/4 = 1.0 by construction and EVERY
  idx+VIX+leadership day becomes EXPANSION (49 vs 30 today). The other
  three legs are the only floor; the breadth leg contributes nothing
  under heavy dropout.
- Frequency in production: NOT measurable retroactively. The fabrication
  this PRD removes logs at DEBUG (not persisted); audit.jsonl is
  notification-transport only; no artifact records per-run quote
  coverage. The invisibility of the failure mode is itself part of the
  F-02 case, but it means real-world dropout frequency must be assumed
  nonzero (yfinance single-symbol outages are routine), not estimated.
