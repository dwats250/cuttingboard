WHY NOW
A missing/NaN previous_close or pct_change becomes a fabricated 0.0 (valid-looking calm) at two layers, silently disarming the kill-switch pct legs, CHAOTIC override, and regime vote coloring; both layers must fail loud instead.

MAX EXPECTED DELTA

GOAL
Make a missing/NaN previous_close or pct_change fail loud (fetch failure /
normalization dropout) instead of fabricating a calm-looking 0.0, so the
kill-switch pct legs, CHAOTIC override, and regime votes cannot be silently
disarmed by absent data.

CHARACTERIZATION (Gate A addition, run 2026-07-18, pre-implementation)

Question: post-F-02, a non-halt tradable with missing data leaves
valid_quotes instead of counting as a fabricated non-advancing 0.0 vote.
Breadth (regime.py _detect_expansion) divides advancing/total over the
symbols PRESENT, so a dropout shrinks the denominator and can RAISE the
ratio. EXPANSION now gates a live candidate-promotion path (PRD-259/260).
Quantify the loosening on real data before writing the fix.

Method: replicated _detect_expansion's four legs exactly (index alignment,
VIX <= -1%, breadth >= 0.70, leadership >= 2 of the 4 in-universe leaders
at >= +1.5%) over the real OHLCV cache (data/cache, 23 symbols), 247
trading days with complete data, 2025-07-11 .. 2026-07-06. Tradable
universe = 16 symbols; 14 are non-halt (all but SPY/QQQ) and can drop out
silently. Adversarial dropout = removing only genuinely non-advancing
non-halt symbols (the worst case; also the case where true full-data
breadth says no).

Results:
- EXPANSION fires today on 30/247 days. The other three legs pass with
  breadth the SOLE blocker on 19 further days.
- Adversarial dropouts needed to flip those 19 days:
  N=1 flips 6 days; N<=2 flips 10; N<=4 flips 16; N<=6 flips 19 (all).
- ONE dark symbol is enough to inflate EXPANSION frequency by +6 days/yr
  (+20% over today's 30), on days where TRUE full-data breadth fails.
- Realistic single-symbol outages flip real days each: USO 5, META 4,
  AMZN 4, PAAS 3, AAPL 3, GLD/SLV/GDX/XLE 2 each, IWM/NVDA/COIN 1 each.
- Unbounded in the limit: with maximal dropout (survivors = SPY, QQQ +
  2 advancing leaders) breadth is 4/4 = 1.0 by construction and EVERY
  idx+VIX+leadership day becomes EXPANSION (49 vs 30 today). The other
  three legs are the only floor; the breadth leg contributes nothing
  under heavy dropout.
- Frequency in production: NOT measurable retroactively. The fabrication
  this PRD removes logs at DEBUG (not persisted); audit.jsonl is
  notification-transport only; no artifact records per-run quote
  coverage. The invisibility of the failure mode is itself part of the
  F-02 case, but it means real-world dropout frequency must be assumed
  nonzero (yfinance single-symbol outages are routine), not estimated.

OPEN GATE RULING (STOP condition hit -- operator decision required)

Per Gate A: "if two symbols missing opens EXPANSION, STOP and report."
Measured: ONE symbol missing opens it on 6 days/yr. Options:
(a) RECOMMENDED: fold a fixed-denominator breadth into this PRD --
    _detect_expansion divides by the configured tradable-universe size
    (16), not len(present quotes). A dropout then counts non-advancing
    for breadth, exactly today's suppressive direction, and the
    loosening never exists. ~3 LOC in regime.py + red test. This is NOT
    the Tier-4 quorum floor (minimum-coverage floor on regime
    confidence/posture overall), which stays a separate PRD.
(b) Ship the Tier-4 quorum floor first (or jointly) as a hard
    prerequisite -- larger scope, pulls the recommendation forward.
(c) Ship F-02 as-is and accept the loosening -- contradicts the Gate A
    framing ("won't ship a fix that loosens a live gate").
