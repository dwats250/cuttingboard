REQUIREMENTS

R1 — Conditional render on a numeric freshness contract
The renderer emits the macro-awareness line only when status=SHOCK AND the snapshot is
fresh. Freshness: max age = 48h (tunable constant), compared in UTC against render time,
boundary inclusive (age <= 48h renders). generated_at missing, non-tz, or unparseable ->
treat as not fresh (no render). generated_at more than +5 minutes in the future (clock
skew) -> not fresh (no render). Otherwise the renderer emits nothing.

FAIL: anything renders when status=QUIET, when the snapshot is missing, when generated_at is
malformed/future-skewed, or when age > 48h.

R2 — Full-schema consumer-side rejection (defense in depth)
Before rendering, the renderer independently validates the COMPLETE R3 contract from
PRD-187: exact top-level keys; status/shock consistency; shock exact keys; event_type in the
fixed enum; source_url domain in the configured allowlist; tz-valid timestamps;
source_excerpt within the length cap. Any violation -> no render (degrade to baseline). The
renderer never trusts the producer's write alone.

FAIL: the renderer renders from a snapshot that violates any top-level or shock field, enum,
URL-domain, timestamp, length, or extra-key rule.

R3 — Ephemeral single line, no column, byte-identical baseline
The render is one line co-located with the macro tape (Q2 region); no standing column or
panel. The QUIET / stale / missing / invalid render is byte-identical to the no-feature
baseline. Every stale/invalid suppression logs an explicit reason (no silent fallback).

FAIL: a standing column/panel is introduced; OR the no-render output differs from the
no-feature baseline; OR a suppression occurs with no logged reason.

R4 — Read-only, no coupling
The renderer reads the snapshot only: no producer import, no decision-surface read or write.

FAIL: the renderer imports the producer or reads/writes any decision surface.

R5 — Safe output
Rendered text is HTML-escaped and length-capped; source_url is rendered as the citation.

FAIL: rendered content is unescaped or exceeds the cap.

R6 — Byte-identical UI regeneration; section order intact
ui/dashboard.html and ui/index.html are regenerated by the canonical generator (not
hand-edited), and tests/test_dash_core.py section-order assertions pass.

FAIL: ui/* is hand-edited; OR ui/dashboard.html and ui/index.html differ; OR a
test_dash_core section-order assertion fails.

R7 — Scheduled activation
The daily pre-market cron is added to .github/workflows/macro_awareness.yml (alongside the
existing workflow_dispatch), making this the producer's first live cadence. The force-add
allowlist (two paths only), validation gate, and push contract from PRD-187 are unchanged.

FAIL: the cron lands before this PRD ships; OR adding the cron alters the two-path force-add
allowlist, the pre-commit validation gate, or the push contract.
